our practice

  • we're punctual: we look up the address and commute ahead of time and we always work in a minimum 20-minute buffer into our travel time.
  • we smile: we know that first impressions are everything & just one smile can make the difference between a good hospitality experience or a bad one.
  • we clarify: clear is kind. we repeat any instructions given to us in our own words so we and the client can be sure we're on the same page!
  • we're attentive: we anticipate needs before they arise, always keeping an eye on the bigger picture.
  • we're adaptable and high-agency: every event is unique, and we pride ourselves on our ability to think on our feet, be proactively helpful and adjust to any situation.
  • we keep things light: remember, it's never that serious (except when it is). We maintain professionalism while injecting fun and spontaneity into our work.
